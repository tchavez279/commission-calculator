About Us

Built for sales people, by people who've worked commission.

My Commission Calculator was created out of frustration with spreadsheets and back-of-napkin math. We wanted a tool that was instant, honest, and actually reflected how commission works in the real world.

Our Story

Commission-based pay is one of the most common compensation structures in the world — and one of the most misunderstood. Most salespeople we spoke to couldn't accurately forecast their own earnings more than a week in advance. They were relying on rough mental math, cluttered spreadsheets, or just waiting for their paycheck to see what came out.

We built My Commission Calculator to solve that. Whether you're a real estate agent trying to figure out your split on a $600,000 listing, an insurance rep calculating residuals, a SaaS account executive modelling your accelerators, or a retail worker adding up unit bonuses and protection plan payouts — this tool handles it all in seconds.

The calculator launched as a simple one-page tool and has grown into a full suite covering basic calculations, detailed pay breakdowns, tiered commission structures, quota accelerators, team splits, tax estimation, and annual income projections.

Our Principles

🔒

Privacy first

Every calculation runs entirely in your browser. We never see your numbers. No accounts, no data collection, no servers storing your financial information. What you type stays on your screen.

⚡

Speed over complexity

Results update instantly as you type. There are no submit buttons, no loading spinners, no page reloads. We designed the interface to get out of your way and give you the answer as fast as possible.

🎯

Real-world accuracy

Commission structures in the real world are messy. Tiered brackets, quota multipliers, team splits, clawbacks, overtime, add-on products — we've built all of it in because that's what salespeople actually deal with.

💸

Always free

This tool is and will always be free to use. No premium tier, no feature paywalls, no "sign up to save your results." The calculator is supported by non-intrusive advertising, and that's it.
